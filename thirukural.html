Kural #1
அகர முதல எழுத்தெல்லாம் · Aram · Virtue
அகர முதல எழுத்தெல்லாம் ஆதி பகவன் முதற்றே உலகு.
English Rendering
As the letter A is the first of all letters, so the eternal God is first in the world.
Elucidation
Just as every word begins with the letter 'A', the universe and all existence begins with and is rooted in God — the primordial source of creation.
